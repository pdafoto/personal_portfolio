About Me

I'm Nicolás, and for over 15 years I've worked as an architecture-specialized photographer and managed my own business projects. During this time, I've learned to be responsible, organized, solution-oriented, and always focused on results.

In recent years, I've decided to pivot my career and focus on the tech sector. I've completed a Master's in Artificial Intelligence and currently study ASIR and Cybersecurity. I also went through 42 Madrid, where I built a solid foundation in programming and a very practical mindset for learning, collaborating, and solving technical problems.

This portfolio showcases some of the projects I'm developing during my training. My goal is to continue growing in IT and bring value from a hybrid profile: technical, analytical, and with extensive prior experience in management and entrepreneurship.
